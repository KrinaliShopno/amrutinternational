Frequently Answered & Questions

Welcome, how can we assist you? We ensure that your information is protected securely. Below are standard queries and answers.

Are Ayurvedic products safe to use?

Ayurvedic products are safe to use when manufactured by reputable companies complying with strict quality standards and used as directed. At Amrut International, we strictly follow ISO and GMP guidelines and use 100% natural, tested herbs to ensure maximum safety and effectiveness.

How can I find a qualified Ayurvedic practitioner?

To find a qualified practitioner, look for professionals who have completed formal Ayurvedic university degrees (like B.A.M.S. in India) and are registered with reputable medical boards or Ayurvedic practitioner councils. Seeking recommendations from certified healthcare providers is also highly recommended.

What types of Ayurvedic & Hygiene products does Amrut International offer?

We supply a wide assortment across 5 key categories:

  • Health & Nutraceuticals (Moringa extracts, Tulsi drops, Whey protein)
  • Herbal Cosmetics (Onion Shampoos, Hair Oils, Keratin range, Hair serums, Beard oils)
  • Sanitary Pads (Premium cotton napkins with Anion chips)
  • Surgical Products (Sanitizers, 3-ply surgical masks)
  • Water Care (Amrut-jal Alkaline Water Ionizers)
Can I purchase Amrut International's products for personal use?

Amrut International operates primarily as a wholesale supplier and global exporter. However, we do accommodate smaller individual direct purchases for personal use through our sales desk. Please contact our Team Sales via the WhatsApp widget for purchase inquiries.

Does Amrut International export its products worldwide?

Yes, we are a registered exporter and supply our products to international importers worldwide. We have verified networks and cargo shipping associations to deliver bulk shipments to your destination port efficiently.
