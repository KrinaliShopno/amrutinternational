Who We Are

The Story Behind Amrut International

Amrut International, established in the year 2017 at Surat (Gujarat, India), is a well-established supplier and exporter of Ayurveda preparations, beauty cosmetics, and hygiene products. Racing ahead since the time of our inception, we have carved out a remarkable name in both domestic and international markets.

We specialize in adapting modern scientific research with Ayurvedic formulation standards. We closely follow GMP and ISO 9001:2015 guidelines to manage a professionally driven production channel. By only utilizing natural materials, we ensure safe, chemical-free, and highly effectual results for skincare, haircare, and personal hygiene.

Apart from this, our core strength lies in maintaining strict shipment timelines, providing cost-effective private labeling packages, and maintaining transparency in all our dealings.

Production Facility

Our Manufacturing & Infrastructure

We are backed by a state-of-the-art infrastructural setup spreading across a large industrial area in Surat, Gujarat. To streamline all commercial operations, we have segmented our facility into distinct wings, managed by senior department heads:

  • R&D Laboratories: Responsible for blending traditional wisdom with scientific testing.
  • Formulation Unit: Housing clean room spaces and automated filling machines for cosmetic gels, liquids, oils, and capsules.
  • Quality Assurance: Reviewing raw supplies and finished batches against micro-bacterial specifications.
  • Warehousing & Packing: Climate-controlled space for packaging materials and stock keeping to enable immediate supply dispatch.
