Milestones

Journey of Amrut International

Learn about our growth, values, and how we expanded from a local supply startup in 2017 to an international supplier of organic products.

2017

Company Inception

Amrut International was established in Surat, Gujarat (India) with a primary mission of promoting authentic Ayurvedic preparations and supplying essential organic healthcare ingredients. We started by building a network of certified herbal farmers.

2019

Supplying Excellence

We expanded our supply capacity, maintaining strict delivery timelines and implementing comprehensive quality testing. By providing cost-effective raw extracts and finished cosmetics formulations, we positioned our brand as a reliable partner for companies across India.

2021

R&D and Formulation Launch

We established our dedicated Research & Development and Formulation units for Ayurvedic, herbal cosmetics, and hygiene products. This allowed us to launch specialized custom private label services for global wellness brands, ensuring strict GMP compliance.

2023

Global Export Operations

Amrut International achieved global export registration. We successfully deployed international distribution channels, shipping Ayurvedic supplements, cosmetics, Anion chip sanitary napkins, and water care systems to distributors and importers worldwide.

Amrut International

Established in the year 2017 in Surat, Gujarat, Amrut International has become a world-renowned exporter and supplier of Ayurvedic preparations, premium beauty cosmetics, and personal hygiene products.
